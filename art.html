Gia Valente is a non-binary installation artist based in Seattle. Their immersive art installations use saturated color, repetition, reflection, and optical illusion to create visually overwhelming spaces that inspire interaction and play. Gia has created site-specific installation work for the Museum of Pop Culture, Town Hall Seattle, Gay City, King Street Station, and at locally-organized underground events.
